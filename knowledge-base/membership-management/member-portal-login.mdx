Access the Member Portal to [change your plan](https://app.generalreferral.com/kb/membership-management/manage-membership) or [update your payment method](https://app.generalreferral.com/kb/membership-management/update-payment-method):

[**https://app.generalreferral.com/members**](https://app.generalreferral.com/members)

Click on the button labeled: "**Member Portal**"

![Member Portal Login Pn](/images/member-portal-login.png)

You may choose to keep the one-time password functionality, which will send you a unique code by email or SMS each time you login, or you may set a permanent password once you've successfully logged in.

To set a permanent password for the member portal, you need to login to the portal using a one-time password. Next, select **Account information** \> **Set up Password**.
