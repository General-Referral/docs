If you're a New Jersey referral agent and can't access your **Qualifying Questionnaire**, it's likely because you don't have your **PIN**.

## Where to Find Your PIN

You should have received an email from the **New Jersey Real Estate Commission** around **April 1st** with your PIN and instructions for completing your renewal questionnaire online.

<Warning>
  If you didn't receive this email or can't find it, don't worryâ€”you can reset your PIN using the steps below.
</Warning>

## How to Reset Your PIN

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to NJ REC Login Page">
    Visit https://www-dobi.state.nj.us/DOBI_RELOLSRV/LicenseeLogin
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click PIN Reset Link">
    Look for and click: **"Click Here If You Do Not Know Your PIN"**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Required Information">
    You'll need:

    - License reference number
    - Last name (as on license)
    - Date of birth
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow Reset Instructions">
    Complete the prompts to reset your PIN
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Still Can't Access the System?

If resetting your PIN doesn't work, you'll need to **call the NJ Real Estate Commission** directly:

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="NJ Real Estate Commission" icon="phone">
    - **Phone**: (609) 292-7272
    - **Email**: [relic@dobi.nj.gov](mailto:relic@dobi.nj.gov)
    - **Hours**: Business days, 8:30 AM - 4:30 PM

    Let them know you're trying to complete your license renewal and cannot retrieve your PIN.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common Issues and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PIN Reset Not Working">
    **Possible Issues:**

    - Information doesn't match license records
    - System maintenance or technical issues
    - Name changes since original licensing

    **Solution:** Call NJ REC directly for assistance
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="License Number Not Recognized">
    **Check These:**

    - Ensure you're using complete license number
    - Verify no extra spaces or characters
    - Try with and without dashes or periods

    **If Still Issues:** Use the license search tool first to verify your exact number
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="System Says License Not Found">
    **Possible Causes:**

    - License may be inactive
    - Recent transfer still processing
    - Database not updated

    **Action:** Contact General Referral support to verify your license status
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Prevention for Next Time

<Info>
  **Save Your Information:** Once you successfully access the system, save your login credentials and PIN in a secure location for future renewals.
</Info>

### Tips for Future Renewals:

- Save the April 1st PIN email in a dedicated folder
- Add renewal dates to your calendar
- Keep license information easily accessible
- Update contact info if you move or change email
