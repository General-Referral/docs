  <Tab title="License">
    ### License Management
    
    **Key Dates:**
    - License renewal: June 30th of odd years
    - Qualifying questionnaire: By May 15th
    - State fees charged: First week of June
    
    <Warning>
      Missing renewal deadlines can result in license deactivation and additional fees.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Billing">
    ### Billing Information
    
    **When You're Charged:**
    - Upon initial signup
    - On membership anniversary
    - During state license renewal (biennial)
    
    **Payment Methods:**
    - Credit/debit cards
    - ACH bank transfer
    - Automatic renewal enabled

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I login to the Member Portal?">
    Visit [app.generalreferral.com/members](https://app.generalreferral.com/members) and click "Member Portal". You can use one-time password via email/SMS or set up a permanent password.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="When am I charged for renewal?">
    Membership renewals are charged on your anniversary date. State license renewals are charged in June of odd-numbered years.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes! You can upgrade immediately with prorated pricing. Downgrades take effect at your next renewal date. No penalties for changes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I can't access my NJ questionnaire?">
    You'll need your PIN from the April 1st email. If you don't have it, visit the [NJ REC website](https://www-dobi.state.nj.us/DOBI_RELOLSRV/LicenseeLogin) and click "Click Here If You Do Not Know Your PIN".
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I switch to full-service?">
    Review the [CE requirements chart](https://www.generalreferral.com/nj-referral-license-change-chart) to determine what continuing education you'll need to complete.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
