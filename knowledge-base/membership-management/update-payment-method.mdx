## Step-by-Step Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Member Portal">
    Navigate to [app.generalreferral.com/members](https://app.generalreferral.com/members)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Member Portal">
    Select the "Member Portal" button on the page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In">
    Use your one-time password (sent via email/SMS) or permanent password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment Info">
    Find payment settings and enter your new payment method
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    Confirm your updates and verify the information is correct
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Setting Up Portal Access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="One-Time Password (Default)">
    **How it Works:**

    - Enter your email or phone number
    - Receive unique code via email or SMS
    - Use code to access portal each time

    **Benefits:**

    - No password to remember
    - Enhanced security
    - Works even if you forget login details
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Permanent Password (Optional)">
    **Setup Process:**

    1. Log in using one-time password first
    2. Navigate to **Account Information**
    3. Select **Set up Password**
    4. Create and confirm your permanent password

    **Benefits:**

    - Faster login for frequent users
    - No need to check email/SMS each time
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Important Timing Considerations

<Warning>
  **License at Risk?** If your license renewal deadline is approaching and you have payment issues, **strongly recommend using a credit or debit card** instead of ACH. ACH payments can take up to 5 days to clear, which might cause renewal delays.
</Warning>

### Critical Deadlines

- **Membership renewals:** Update before your anniversary date
- **State license renewals:** Update by early June for NJ renewals
- **License transfers:** Update immediately for urgent transfers

## Troubleshooting Payment Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment Declined">
    **Common Causes:**

    - Insufficient funds
    - Expired card
    - Incorrect billing address
    - Bank security holds

    **Solutions:**

    - Verify account balance
    - Check card expiration date
    - Confirm billing address matches bank records
    - Contact your bank about holds
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't Access Portal">
    **If you can't log in:**

    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Try different browser or incognito mode
    - Ensure email/phone number is correct
    - Check spam folder for login codes

    **Still need help?** [Contact our support team](/contact)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Old Payment Method Charged">
    **If wrong card was charged:**

    - Update payment method immediately
    - Contact support to verify changes took effect
    - Monitor future charges to confirm correction
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Automatic Renewals

<Note>
  **Set and Forget:** Once your payment method is updated, all future renewals will process automatically on their due dates, provided your payment method remains valid.
</Note>

### What Processes Automatically:

- Annual or monthly membership renewals
- Biennial state license renewals (NJ)
- Any applicable state transfer fees

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How quickly do payment updates take effect?">
    Changes are immediate for credit and debit cards. ACH changes may take 1-2 business days to verify.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a different name on the payment method?">
    The name on the payment method should match your member account. Contact support if you need to use a different name.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will I get confirmation of payment method changes?">
    Yes, you'll receive email confirmation when payment methods are successfully updated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I set up automatic payments from my bank?">
    Our system handles automatic renewals once you provide payment information. You don't need to set up separate automatic payments from your bank.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
