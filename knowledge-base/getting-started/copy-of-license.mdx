---
title: "How do I get a copy of my real estate license?"
description: "Description of your new file."
---

From the [State of New Jersey website](https://www.state.nj.us/dobi/division_rec/licensing/online_Instructions/index.html):

> _Real Estate Licensees: All licensees have the ability to generate their own real estate license as an Adobe Acrobat PDF document. You have the option of saving the document for printing or having it emailed directly to you for display on a cell phone, tablet, laptop, or other electronic device. This includes referral agents, salespersons, brokersalespersons and brokers of record._
