General Referral is a great option for new agents looking for an introduction to the business and a low-cost alternative to hanging their license at a full-service broker.

<Info>
  **Time Sensitive:** After completing your pre-licensure coursework, New Jersey requires that you pass the state licensing exam and find a broker sponsor within one year of your class completion.
</Info>

## Required Documents

After signing up with General Referral, you'll upload your documents directly from your account — no emailing required:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    After checkout you'll land on the signup success page. Create your account (or sign in) to unlock the document upload tool on that page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload each document">
    Upload a PDF, JPG, or PNG (max 10MB per file) for each required document. You can also attach any additional documents you'd like to share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We review your uploads">
    Our team reviews each document and emails you the outcome. If something can't be accepted (for example, a blurry photo), you'll get the reason and can upload a corrected copy from the same page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Identity Verification">
    ### Government-Issued Photo ID
    - State driver's license, OR
    - Federal government ID, OR  
    - Valid passport
    
    <Note>Must be current and clearly show your photo and information</Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Exam Verification">
    ### Copy of Exam Pass Notice
    - Official notification from testing center
    - Shows your name and pass status
    - Includes exam date and score (if provided)

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Tracking Your Document Status

The upload page shows each required document as **Outstanding**, **Pending review**, **Approved**, or **Rejected** (with the reason), so you always know what's left to do. Once both required documents are approved, you'll receive a confirmation email and no further document action is needed.

<Note>If you run into trouble uploading, you can still email your documents to [support@generalreferral.com](mailto:support@generalreferral.com) and we'll take care of them.</Note>

## Fingerprinting Requirements

<Warning>
  **Critical Step:** You must complete fingerprinting as part of the licensing process. General Referral does not need confirmation of your fingerprint status - this is handled directly with the state.
</Warning>

### Fingerprinting Process:
1. **Schedule Appointment**: Use the [state's fingerprint portal](https://www.state.nj.us/dobi/division_rec/licensing/fingerprint.html)
2. **Attend Appointment**: Bring required identification
3. **Pay Required Fees**: Typically $50-75 for processing
4. **Results Processing**: State receives results automatically

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the approval process take?">
    After submitting all documents and completing fingerprinting, approval typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on state processing times and background check results.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if my fingerprints are rejected?">
    If there are issues with fingerprint quality, you'll need to be re-printed. The state will notify you directly of any problems.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I start working before final approval?">
    No, you must wait for official license approval before engaging in any real estate activities, including referrals.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I miss the one-year deadline?">
    If you don't find a sponsor within one year of class completion, you may need to retake pre-licensure education. Contact the state for specific guidance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
