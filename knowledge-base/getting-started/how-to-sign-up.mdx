## What Happens After Signup?

Once you complete checkout, you'll see a detailed list of required next steps based on your specific situation. Here's what to expect:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Active License">
    **If You Have an Existing, Active License:**
    
    After signing up with General Referral, you will need to:
    1. Contact your current broker
    2. Request they release your license to the state
    3. Wait for confirmation (usually 1-2 business days)
    
    <Note>
      Your current broker must release your license before we can sponsor you.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Just Passed Exam">
    **If You Recently Passed Your Exam:**
    
    You'll need to ensure you've met all state requirements:
    - Complete fingerprinting (if not already done)
    - Submit required documentation to General Referral
    - We'll handle the state application on your behalf
    
    <Tip>
      Check out our guide on [requirements after passing your exam](/knowledge-base/getting-started/requirements-after-exam) for detailed information.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Inactive License">
    **If Your License is Currently Inactive:**
    
    Good news! No further action regarding your previous broker is needed. After signing up:
    - We'll reactivate your license with the state
    - You'll receive confirmation once complete
    - Start making referrals immediately after activation
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the signup process take?">
    The online signup typically takes 5-10 minutes. License transfer timing depends on your current broker and state processing, usually 1-3 business days.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and ACH/bank transfers. For urgent license transfers, we recommend using a credit or debit card.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I sign up if I'm with another broker?">
    Yes! Just complete our signup process, then request your current broker to release your license. We'll handle the rest once it's released.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I need help during signup?">
    [Contact our support team](https://app.generalreferral.com/contact) for assistance. We're here to help make your transition smooth.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
