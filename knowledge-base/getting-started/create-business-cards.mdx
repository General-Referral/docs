## Compliance Requirements

<Warning>
  **NJ Legal Requirement:** According to New Jersey Real Estate License Act regulations, business cards must indicate that you are a referral agent.
</Warning>

### Required Elements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Referral Agent Designation">
    Must use one of these phrases:

    - "Referral Agent"
    - "Referral Associate"

    This designation must be clearly visible on the card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Brokerage Information">
    Include:

    - General Referral company name
    - Your name as licensed agent
    - Contact information (phone, email)
    - General Referral branding elements
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Professional Appearance">
    Maintain:

    - Clean, professional design
    - Readable fonts and sizing
    - Consistent branding
    - High-quality printing
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Design Best Practices

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Information Hierarchy">
    ### Essential Information Order

    1. Your name (prominent)
    2. "Referral Agent" designation
    3. Contact phone number
    4. Email address
    5. General Referral branding
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Visual Guidelines">
    ### Design Elements

    - Use General Referral brand colors
    - Maintain consistent font styles
    - Ensure adequate white space
    - Include professional photo (optional)
    - Keep design clean and uncluttered
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Contact Information">
    ### What to Include

    - Primary phone number
    - Professional email address
    - Website (if applicable)
    - Social media handles (optional)
    - QR code for contact info (optional)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Missing Required Designation">
    **Problem:** Forgetting to include "Referral Agent" or "Referral Associate"

    **Solution:** Always double-check that the designation is clearly visible before printing
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Misleading Services">
    **Problem:** Implying you provide full-service real estate activities

    **Solution:** Keep messaging focused on referral services only
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poor Quality Printing">
    **Problem:** Using low-quality paper or printing that looks unprofessional

    **Solution:** Invest in quality cardstock and professional printing
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
