## Additional State Fees

The only other costs you incur are fees charged by your state licensing body:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="One-Time Fees">
    - **License Transfer Fee**: When you move your license to General Referral
    - **License Type Change**: If switching your license to the referral agent type
    - **Reactivation Fee**: If your license is currently inactive and you've missed the last license renewal
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Recurring Fees">
    - **License Renewal**: Biennial fee (every 2 years in NJ)
    - **Continuing Education**: Not required for NJ referral agents\!
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  We collect state fees from you and pay them on your behalf.
</Note>
