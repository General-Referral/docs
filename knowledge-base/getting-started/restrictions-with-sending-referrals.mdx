---
title: "Do you restrict where I can send my referrals?"
---

<Note>
  No, we do not restrict where you can send your referrals.
</Note>

We encourage developing relationships with third-party brokers and salespeople. Many of our competitors impose restrictions that can lead to a loss of trust and business.

If you are new to the business and need help finding a qualified agent to work with, we have a network of professional agents we can introduce you to.
