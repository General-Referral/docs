## Step-by-Step Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the State Website">
    Go to the [NJ License Search portal](https://www.state.nj.us/dobi/division_rec/licensing/online_Instructions/licSearch.html)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Information">
    You can search by:
    - License number (if you know part of it)
    - Last name and first name
    - Business name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Results">
    Find your record and note your complete license number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save for Future Reference">
    Write down or save your license number for future use
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You'll Need

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Personal Information">
    - Your legal first and last name
    - Exactly as it appears on your license
    - Middle initial if used during licensing
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Alternative Search Methods">
    If name search doesn't work:
    - Try variations of your name
    - Check if your name has changed since licensing
    - Contact the NJ Real Estate Commission for assistance
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## License Information Details

Once you find your license, you'll see:
- **License Number**: Your unique identifier
- **License Type**: Should show "Salesperson (Referral)" 
- **Status**: Should be "Active"
- **Expiration Date**: Your renewal deadline
- **Broker Information**: General Referral, LLC

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't Find Your License">
    **Possible Reasons:**
    - License application still pending
    - Name spelled differently than expected
    - License may be inactive or expired
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Try different name variations
    - Contact NJ REC at (609) 292-7272
    - Check with General Referral support
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Shows Wrong Broker">
    **If your license shows a different broker:**
    - Your previous broker may not have released your license
    - Transfer may still be processing
    - Contact General Referral support for assistance
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why You Need Your License Number

Your license number is required for:
- Referral agreements and contracts
- State renewal applications
- Professional documentation
- Compliance verification
- Member portal access
