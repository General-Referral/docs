## Our Only Requirement

When you select an agent for your referral, our only requirement is that **they are qualified**:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="License Verification">
    ### Required Qualifications
    - Active real estate license in good standing
    - Proper licensing in the target market/state
    - No recent disciplinary actions
    - Current association with licensed brokerage
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Professional Standards">
    ### Recommended Qualities
    - Experience in the property type/market
    - Strong communication skills
    - Professional reputation
    - Client satisfaction track record
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Referral Fee Guidelines

<Info>
  **Market Standard:** 25% is a good rule of thumb for most residential referrals, but you're free to negotiate based on value and market conditions.
</Info>

### Factors Affecting Referral Fees

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Higher Fees (30-35%)">
    - Highly qualified, motivated clients
    - Luxury or high-value properties
    - Exclusive or difficult-to-find clients
    - Strong ongoing relationship potential
    - Multiple transaction opportunities
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Standard Fees (25-30%)">
    - Typical qualified buyers/sellers
    - Standard market transactions
    - Good client preparation and qualification
    - Clear transaction parameters
    - Professional referral presentation
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Lower Fees (20-25%)">
    - Challenging clients or situations
    - Highly competitive markets
    - New agent relationships
    - Complex or uncertain transactions
    - Limited agent availability
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Building Your Agent Network

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start Local">
    Begin building relationships with agents in your immediate area:
    - Attend local real estate events
    - Join professional networking groups
    - Connect with top-producing agents
    - Research market leaders online
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Expand Geographically">
    As you grow, develop partnerships in other markets:
    - Research agents in target locations
    - Use referral networks and platforms
    - Leverage existing agent connections
    - Attend regional conferences
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Quality Over Quantity">
    Focus on building strong relationships with fewer, better agents:
    - Consistent communication
    - Fair referral fees
    - Professional client preparation
    - Mutual respect and trust
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
