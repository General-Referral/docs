## Commission Information

<Info>
  **Typical Referral Fees:** Most referrals earn 25% of the gross commission due to the side referred. You're free to negotiate the best rate the market will bear when you place the referral yourself.
</Info>

### Payment Timeline

<Timeline>
  <TimelineItem title="Transaction Closes">
    The property sale or purchase is completed
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem title="Commission Sent">
    Receiving broker sends payment by mail to General Referral
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem title="Notification">
    You receive email confirmation of payment receipt by General Referral
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem title="Submit Disbursement">
    You complete the online disbursement form
  </TimelineItem>

  <TimelineItem title="Direct Deposit">
    Funds arrive in your account (typically 4 business days)
  </TimelineItem>
</Timeline>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I refer myself or family members?">
    Absolutely! It's one of the best benefits of holding your license. You can refer yourself or immediate family members and earn the same commission as any other referral.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you restrict where I send referrals?">
    No! Unlike many competitors, we don't restrict where you send referrals. Build relationships with any qualified agents you trust.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I change a referral agreement?">
    Complete a new agreement and note in the "Notes" section that it replaces the previous agreement dated [date]. Contact support if you need help voiding the old agreement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I find past referrals?">
    Search your email for "Completed: Please Sign this Referral Agreement" to find executed agreements sent to your email address.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
