All agents licensed with General Referral who earn income in the calendar year receive a 1099 tax form through our payroll provider. Typically, the form is available at the end of January of the following year.

<Note>
  We currently use Square Payroll to process payments and issue tax forms. To download your 1099 tax form, please log into your Payroll Provider account: [Square](https://squareup.com/us/en)
</Note>

### **Downloading Your 1099 Tax Form from Square Payroll**

Some have reported issues downloading the 1099 tax form from the Square Payroll website. If you are having trouble, please install the Square Team App for Team Members: [Square Team App](https://squareup.com/help/us/en/article/7060-square-team-app-for-team-members)

For more help using Square Payroll, please see their help article: [Setting up Square Payroll](https://squareup.com/help/us/en/article/5336-square-payroll-employee-and-contractor-sign-up)
