All agents licensed with General Referral who earn income in the calendar year receive a 1099 tax form through our payroll provider. Typically, the form is available at the end of January of the following year.

<Note>
  We currently use Gusto to process payments and issue tax forms. To download your 1099 tax form, please log into your Payroll Provider account: [Gusto](https://login.gusto.com/)
</Note>

### **Downloading Your 1099 Tax Form from Gusto**

For more information on how to obtain your 1099 Tax Form, here's a [help article](https://support.gusto.com/article/106622213100000/View-my-1099-NEC-as-a-contractor).

For more help using Gusto, please see their help article: [Contractors](https://support.gusto.com/#Contractors)
