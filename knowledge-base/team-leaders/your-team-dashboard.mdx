Your dashboard is the home screen of your team portal. It gives you an at-a-glance
view of how your team is growing.

**What you'll see:**

- **Active members** — the number of agents on your team with an active subscription.
- **New this month** — how many agents joined since the start of the current month.

**Tips:**

- The dashboard updates automatically as agents join — there's nothing to refresh
  or maintain.
- For a member-by-member breakdown (including who has an active vs. canceled
  subscription), open the **Roster**.

If your member count looks off, check the Roster first — it's the source of truth
for who's on your team.
