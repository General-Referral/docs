The Roster lists every agent on your team and how they're doing. Open it from the
team portal.

**Each agent shows:**

- **Name** and **Email**
- **Joined** date
- **Attribution** — how they joined your team (e.g. Subdomain, Cookie, Admin,
  Migrated)
- **Status** — **Active** or **Canceled** (whether their General Referral
  subscription is current)

**What you can do:**

- **Sort** any column by clicking its header (click again to reverse).
- **Search** by name or email to quickly find someone.
- **Export CSV** to download the full roster for your own records.

**Good to know:**

- The Roster shows membership and subscription status only — not your members'
  individual commissions or private billing details.
- A **Canceled** status means that agent's subscription has lapsed; they may need
  a nudge to re-enroll.
