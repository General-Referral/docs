When one of your agents has a client but wants you to choose who receives the
referral, they can **delegate it to you**. Those referrals land in your
**Referrals** page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/team-leaders/referrals.png" alt="The Referrals page, showing a referral awaiting your assignment and one in progress with signature tracking" />
</Frame>

**How it works:**

1. Your agent starts a referral and picks **"Let my team leader choose."** They
   enter only the client and what the client needs — no receiving agent.
2. You're notified right away (in-app and email — this alert is always on).
3. You open **Referrals → Needs your assignment**, review the client details, and
   fill in the **receiving agent and broker**. You can name yourself in either or
   both roles.

   <Frame>
     <img src="/images/team-leaders/assign-form.png" alt="The assignment form on a delegated referral, with fields for the receiving agent and broker" />
   </Frame>

4. Once you assign it, the referral enters General Referral's normal review and
   signing flow — and your agent is notified it's moving.

**Tracking it:**

- **Needs your assignment** — waiting on you.
- **In progress** — assigned and moving through signatures (you can see who has and
  hasn't signed).
- **Completed / closed** — finished.

**Note:** You'll only see referrals that were *delegated to you* here — not every
referral your members create. If you have questions about a client, contact the
referring agent directly.
