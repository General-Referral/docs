Stay on top of team activity with in-app and email notifications.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/team-leaders/notifications.png" alt="The in-app notification feed, with unread items highlighted and an unread-count badge on the sidebar menu" />
</Frame>

**Two ways you're notified:**

- **In-app feed** — open **Notifications** in the portal. A badge on the menu shows
  how many are unread; "Mark all as read" clears it. The in-app feed is always
  on — every team event lands here.
- **Email** — the same alerts can also be sent to your inbox. You choose which.

**What you're notified about:**

- **A new member joins** your team
- **A member leaves** (their subscription is canceled)

**Turning emails on:**

- Go to **Settings → Email Notifications**.
- Emails are **off by default** — flip on the ones you want. The toggles only
  control email; the in-app feed keeps working either way.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/team-leaders/settings.png" alt="The Settings page with email notification toggles for new-member and member-left alerts" />
</Frame>

**Note:** Some alerts are always on because they need your action — for example,
when a member sends you a referral to assign (see *Delegated Referrals*). Those
can't be turned off.
