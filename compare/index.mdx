## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the commission structure work?">
    With General Referral, you keep 100% of your referral fee less a flat \$295 transaction fee. The typical referral fee is 25–30% of the gross commission due to the side referred. Because our fee is flat (not a percentage), you keep more as the transaction value increases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I choose where my referral goes?">
    Yes — General Referral places no restrictions on where you send referrals. You can refer to any licensed agent at any brokerage, anywhere in the country. Some competitors restrict referrals to their own network or require referrals to go through the employing broker.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does a license transfer take?">
    The entire process typically takes less than one week, though it can take up to 10 business days. Your current broker releases your license (1-2 days), then we sponsor it with the state.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch back to full-service?">
    Yes. If you decide to return to full-service real estate, you can transfer your license to any brokerage. You'll need to catch up on any continuing education credits you missed during your time as a referral agent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are state fees included in the plan price?">
    No. State licensing fees (transfer fees, renewal fees, etc.) are broken out separately during the signup process so you can see exactly what you're paying and why. We collect these fees and pay them to the state on your behalf.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my plan later?">
    Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the comparison data on this page accurate?">
    We make a best effort to keep this information current using publicly available sources. Competitor pricing and policies may change without notice. Last updated: February 2026. If you notice an error, [let us know](https://app.generalreferral.com/contact).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
