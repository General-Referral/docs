NRS Referrals (Berkshire Hathaway - Fox & Roach) is a referral brokerage based in Devon, Pennsylvania — not New Jersey. They offer an 80% commission split on referral fees with a \$150 annual fee. For NJ-licensed agents, the key consideration is that NRS operates from out of state with no New Jersey office, which means you would be working under a broker who is not locally present. If having a local NJ broker and maximizing your per-referral income are priorities, this comparison will help you evaluate both options.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | General Referral | NRS Referrals |
|---|---|---|
| **Commission Split** | 100% (less \$295 flat fee) | 80% |
| **Annual Fee** | \$99–119 | \$150 |
| **NJ Office** | Yes (Midland Park & Holmdel) | No (Devon, PA) |
| **Online Signup** | Yes | No |
| **Referral Restrictions** | None | None stated |
| **Online Referral Portal** | Yes | No |

## Commission Deep Dive

The commission structure is the most impactful difference between these two programs. Here is how each model works on an identical transaction.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Referral Model">
    With General Referral, you keep 100% of your referral commission minus a flat \$295 transaction fee. The fee stays the same regardless of the referral amount.

    **Worked example — \$500K home sale:**

    | Line Item | Amount |
    |---|---|
    | Home sale price | \$500,000 |
    | Gross commission (3%) | \$15,000 |
    | Your referral fee (25%) | \$3,750 |
    | General Referral transaction fee | -\$295 |
    | **Your take-home** | **\$3,455 (92.1%)** |

    Because the fee is flat, your effective percentage improves as the transaction value increases.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="NRS Model">
    With NRS Referrals, you receive 80% of your referral commission. The brokerage retains 20% of each referral fee.

    **Worked example — \$500K home sale:**

    | Line Item | Amount |
    |---|---|
    | Home sale price | \$500,000 |
    | Gross commission (3%) | \$15,000 |
    | Your referral fee (25%) | \$3,750 |
    | NRS share (20%) | -\$750 |
    | **Your take-home** | **\$3,000 (80%)** |

    The percentage-based split means the brokerage's share grows proportionally with the referral amount.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  On this single transaction, General Referral puts **\$455 more** in your pocket. Over multiple referrals per year, this difference compounds quickly.
</Info>

## Technology & Tools

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Referral">
    General Referral provides a modern, digital-first experience for referral agents:

    - **Online referral portal** — Submit and manage referrals from anywhere through a dedicated web-based dashboard
    - **Digital referral submission** — No paperwork, phone calls, or faxes required to place a referral
    - **Electronic agreements via SignWell** — Sign all documents digitally without printing, scanning, or mailing
    - **Commission tracking** — Monitor the status and payout of your referrals in real time
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="NRS Referrals">
    NRS Referrals operates with traditional brokerage processes:

    - **Traditional referral processes** — Referrals are managed through conventional communication channels
    - **No online referral portal** — There is no self-service web platform for submitting or tracking referrals
    - **Standard documentation** — Agreements and paperwork follow typical brokerage workflows
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Who Should Choose General Referral?

General Referral is the stronger choice if you:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Want a Local NJ Broker">
    With offices in both Midland Park and Holmdel, you have a New Jersey-based broker with convenient in-state locations for any support you need.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Want to Maximize Every Referral">
    The flat \$295 fee beats a 20% commission cut on any referral earning more than approximately \$1,475 — which covers the vast majority of real estate referrals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prefer Online Tools">
    You want to submit referrals, sign agreements, and track commissions digitally — all through a self-service online portal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Value Transparent, Predictable Costs">
    A flat \$295 fee per transaction means you always know exactly what you will pay. No percentage math, no surprises as commission amounts change.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
