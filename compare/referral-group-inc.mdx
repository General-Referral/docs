Referral Group Inc (Remax) is a New Jersey-based referral brokerage that offers an 80% commission split on standard referrals and a 90% split on personal transactions. As a closer competitor to General Referral than many traditional brokerages, Referral Group Inc appeals to agents who want a straightforward referral-only model. The key difference comes down to structure: General Referral uses a flat \$295 per-transaction fee instead of a percentage-based split, which means your savings grow as your referral commissions increase.

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | General Referral | Referral Group Inc |
|---|---|---|
| **Commission Split** | 100% (less \$295 flat fee) | 80% (90% for personal transactions) |
| **Annual Fee** | \$99 - \$119 | \$100 |
| **NJ Office** | Yes (Midland Park & Holmdel) | Yes (Hamilton Square) |
| **Online Signup** | Yes | No |
| **Referral Restrictions** | None | None stated |
| **Online Referral Portal** | Yes | No |

## Commission Deep Dive

To see how the two models compare in practice, consider a typical referral scenario: a \$500,000 home with a 3% commission (\$15,000) and a 25% referral fee (\$3,750).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Referral Model">
    With General Referral, you keep 100% of your referral commission minus a flat \$295 transaction fee:

    - **Referral commission earned:** \$3,750
    - **Transaction fee:** \$295
    - **Your net payout:** \$3,455
    - **Effective split:** 92.1%

    The flat fee stays at \$295 regardless of the commission amount, so your effective split improves on higher-value referrals.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Referral Group Inc Model">
    With Referral Group Inc, you receive 80% of your referral commission (or 90% on personal transactions):

    **Standard referral (80% split):**
    - **Referral commission earned:** \$3,750
    - **Brokerage cut (20%):** \$750
    - **Your net payout:** \$3,000
    - **Effective split:** 80%

    **Personal transaction (90% split):**
    - **Referral commission earned:** \$3,750
    - **Brokerage cut (10%):** \$375
    - **Your net payout:** \$3,375
    - **Effective split:** 90%
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
On this \$3,750 referral commission, General Referral puts **\$455 more** in your pocket compared to Referral Group Inc's standard 80% split. Even against the 90% personal transaction rate, General Referral still comes out ahead by **\$80**.
</Info>

<Note>
The General Referral flat fee model beats any percentage-based split on referral commissions above approximately \$1,475. For commissions at or below that threshold, a percentage split may yield a similar or slightly better result — but most real estate referral commissions comfortably exceed that figure.
</Note>

## Technology & Tools

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Referral">
    General Referral provides a modern, digital-first experience for referral agents:

    - **Online referral portal** — Submit and manage referrals from anywhere through a dedicated web-based dashboard
    - **Digital referral submission** — No paperwork or phone calls required to place a referral
    - **Electronic agreements via SignWell** — Sign all documents digitally without printing, scanning, or mailing
    - **Commission tracking** — Monitor the status and payout of your referrals in real time
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Referral Group Inc">
    Referral Group Inc operates with a more traditional brokerage approach:

    - **Traditional brokerage processes** — Referrals are managed through conventional communication channels
    - **No online referral portal** — There is no self-service web platform for submitting or tracking referrals
    - **Standard documentation** — Agreements and paperwork follow typical brokerage workflows
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
